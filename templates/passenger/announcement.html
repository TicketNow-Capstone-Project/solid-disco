IMPORTANT UPDATE

We are pleased to inform the public that the Maasin City Integrated Terminal and Market Complex continues to operate as a central hub for transportation and local commerce. All facilities are fully operational with enhanced safety measures in place.

The terminal operates from 4:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily, providing seamless connectivity across Southern Leyte and neighboring provinces. Our market complex offers fresh local produce and essential goods from 5:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Thank you for your cooperation, and we wish everyone a safe and pleasant journey.
